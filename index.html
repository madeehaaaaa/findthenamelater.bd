Everything About Bangla

This is not a website.

This is not a blog.

This is an archive of breath — of how Bangla was spoken, written, sung, forgotten, and remembered again.

A language does not survive in books.
It survives in mouths.

AOBD exists to document Bangla beyond timelines and exams — beyond “pure” and “impure,” beyond approval.

Here live dialects that never reached print, words spoken by rivers, grammar broken by love, and art painted on moving rickshaws.
